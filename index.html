About

I'm Sjoerd (pronounced shoehrt) — an Applied AI UX Leader based in San Francisco with 15+ years in digital design. Eight of those have been in leadership, and the last three I've spent leading AI-powered product experiences serving millions of users.

I've worked with Google, Meta, Adidas, ING Bank, Burger King, and Zoox — across industries from enterprise AI and autonomous vehicles to fintech and consumer technology. Originally from The Netherlands, I moved to the Bay Area to build a UX studio for MediaMonks and have called San Francisco home ever since.

I hold a bachelor's in Communication and Multimedia Design from the Hogeschool van Amsterdam, and speak both Dutch and English. Always happy to connect over coffee or a good problem to solve.
